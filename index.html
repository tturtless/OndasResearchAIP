Company Thesis
Ondas Inc. (ONDS), now headquartered in West Palm Beach, FL, is executing one of the most aggressive roll-up strategies in small-cap defense: 14+ acquisitions since 2021, with 8 announced or closed in 2026 alone. Through Ondas Autonomous Systems (OAS) — American Robotics, Airobotics, Sentrycs, Roboteam, Apeiro Motion, Mistral, World View, Omnisys, and DZYNE Technologies — the company fields an integrated aerial/ground/counter-UAS/ISR stack, now including a U.S. defense prime contractor (Mistral) with existing DoD program relationships. Ondas Networks continues to deliver the FullMAX (IEEE 802.16t) SDR platform for rail, utilities, and government. Q1 2026 revenue exploded to $50.1M (+1,065% YoY), roughly matching all of FY2025 in a single quarter, and management has raised full-year 2026 guidance twice — from $175M to $390M to now at least $525M — driven by the DZYNE and Omnisys deals plus a $457M pro forma backlog. The company raised approximately $959M in a January 2026 equity offering, giving it over $1.4B in cash and short-term investments as of Q1 2026 to fund continued M&A.
